AI / ML Layer

Forecast demand and make the platform adaptive

The AI/ML layer should read opportunities, staffing requests, applications, mentor engagement, and civic activity to predict demand spikes, supply risks, and the next best actions for candidates and institutions.

Capabilities

Forecast sector-wise demand spikes from platform activity
Estimate projected openings and fill confidence over the next 1 to 2 weeks
Recommend where candidates should focus for better conversion
Warn institutions when demand outpaces candidate supply
Track whether prep and mentor engagement improve readiness

Input Signals

Opportunities, requests, saves, applies, interviews, mentor bookings, and mission joins.

Forecast Outputs

Demand score, projected openings, fill confidence, readiness probability, and shortage alerts.

Model Evolution

Start with heuristic scoring, then move into time-series forecasting and matching models.
