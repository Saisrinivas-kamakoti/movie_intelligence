# 🎬 CineSignal Quick Start Guide

## What is CineSignal?

CineSignal is an AI-powered platform that helps directors, studios, and OTT platforms understand which types of movies and genre combinations perform well in different markets, especially India.

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Maximize Your Score

1. **Genre Selection**:
   - Mix complementary genres (Action + Drama, Thriller + Crime)
   - Avoid too many genres (stick to 2-3)
   - Consider market fit when choosing

2. **Budget Optimization**:
   - High budget for Action/Sci-Fi/Fantasy (needs VFX)
   - Low-Medium for Drama/Thriller/Horror (content-driven)
   - Match budget to genre requirements

3. **Star Power**:
   - Critical for theatrical releases (aim for 7+)
   - Less important for OTT (content can shine)
   - Balances risk in big-budget films

4. **Novelty Factor**:
   - High novelty = better OTT performance
   - Unique concepts cut through noise
   - Innovation attracts streaming audiences

5. **Family Appeal**:
   - High family appeal = broader reach
   - Essential for theatrical success
   - Increases word-of-mouth

6. **Market Targeting**:
   - India-first: Use Hindi/Tamil/Telugu, emotional tones, family appeal
   - Global-first: English, action-heavy, universal themes
   - Pan-India: Star power + regional dubbing

7. **Release Strategy**:
   - Theatrical: Big spectacles, star-driven, family films
   - OTT: Content-driven, niche, thriller/drama
   - Hybrid: Best of both worlds

## 🎯 Common Questions

**Q: Is higher always better?**
A: Not always. A 75+ score with high confidence is better than 90 with low confidence. Context matters.

**Q: Should I change my concept based on predictions?**
A: Use it as guidance, not gospel. Balance market insights with your creative vision.

**Q: What if my concept scores low?**
A: Check recommendations. Small tweaks (casting, tone, budget) can significantly improve scores.

**Q: Which market should I target?**
A: Compare India Fit vs Global Fit. If India Fit is 15+ points higher, focus India-first.

**Q: Theatrical or OTT?**
A: Compare Theatrical vs OTT potential scores. Also consider budget and star power.
